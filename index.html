About Us

SRLC (Scientific Research and Learning Center) is a hub for fostering scientific research and providing high-quality learning opportunities. Under the leadership of Mr. Arjun Dhungana, our headquarters located in Biratnagar, Nepal, strives to create an environment of innovation, discovery, and education.

Our Services

Research Programs

Leading-edge scientific research projects in various fields of science and technology.

Workshops & Training

Interactive learning sessions, training programs, and seminars for students and professionals.

Publications & Journals

Promoting academic writing, publishing research articles, and disseminating knowledge globally.

Teaching & Learning Activities

Interactive Lectures

Engaging classroom sessions focused on practical understanding and scientific applications.

Project-Based Learning

Encouraging students to participate in hands-on projects to develop research and analytical skills.

Collaborations

SRLC proudly collaborates with various national and international institutions to promote advanced research, knowledge exchange, and academic excellence. Through these partnerships, we aim to expand opportunities and contribute significantly to global scientific advancements.
